About Me

As a freshman learner in backend development, my experience has been both challenging and rewarding. I began by grasping foundational concepts such as server-client architecture and the role of databases in web applications. Through hands-on projects, I learned to use languages like Python and JavaScript, experimenting with frameworks such as Flask and Express.js to build simple APIs. I also gained exposure to database management with SQL, allowing me to create and manipulate data effectively. Debugging and troubleshooting became vital skills as I encountered common issues in my code. Overall, this early journey has ignited my passion for backend development, and I am excited to delve deeper into building scalable, efficient applications.
